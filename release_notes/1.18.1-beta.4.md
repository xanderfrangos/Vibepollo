# Vibepollo 1.18.1-beta.4 - 2026-07-19

Notice: Vibepollo may trigger false-positive antivirus alerts. Details and workarounds are documented here: https://github.com/Nonary/Vibepollo/issues/29

## Changes
- Reverted the WGC capture improvements introduced in beta.2 after reports of severe stutter, latency, and encoder timeouts, restoring the earlier conservative multi-copy handoff path.

## Fixes
- Prevented browser streams with intentionally sparse frame cadence from being reset based on callback spacing instead of actual render delay.
